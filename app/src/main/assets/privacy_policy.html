Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PixVault ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the PixVault mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email, and password via Firebase Authentication.
  • Device Storage: Files you choose to import into the app are saved in your device's internal storage and never uploaded to any cloud or third-party server.
  • Optional Data: If you contact us for support, we may collect your name and email address for communication purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality, such as secure login and file storage. We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

3. Data Sharing and Advertising

PixVault does not share your personal data with third parties. We also do not show ads or use advertising SDKs of any kind.

4. Data Security

We use Android’s private internal storage system and optional biometric authentication to keep your files and credentials safe. We never access or share your files without your consent.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at our email below.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to delete all locally stored data on your device.

6. Data Retention

We retain your Firebase authentication data (email, password) as long as your account is active. Locally stored vault files remain on your device until manually deleted or the app is uninstalled.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have, please contact us for immediate deletion.

8. GDPR and Global Compliance

PixVault complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other global data protection laws. Users can request to review, modify, or delete their data by contacting us at the support email provided below.
